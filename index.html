About Me.

About Me.

Hi. I'm Aditya Kundu. I am a student designer that aspires to bring impact to products through design. Working with the startup industry, as a freelance consultant, during my undergrad taught me how design processes could influence product and business decisions. These insights allow me to use design as a strategy rather than being just another deliverable.

Also, I game. A lot. Experiencing games as an active participant provides for a much richer experience compared to being a passive observer in traditional storytelling mediums such as cinema, literature, and music.

Previously interned at Slack, Cowrks, Lollypop Design Studio
